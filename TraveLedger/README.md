# TraveLedger - Complete Accounting & Financial Management Web Application

## Mission Statement
TraveLedger is a complete accounting and financial management web application built specifically for Travel Agencies, Tour Operators, and B2B Travel Agents. It handles everything a modern travel agency needs: tracking flight and hotel sales, managing payments received from customers, settling debts with big suppliers (like airlines and aggregators), logging office expenses, and providing a real-time view of business profits.

---

## Core Features

### 🏢 Agency Workspaces & Admin (Multi-Tenancy)
- **Separate Workspaces:** Multiple agencies can use the app securely, completely separated from each other.
- **Custom Branding:** Customize invoices with your own logo and custom document numbers (like "DELHI-INV-001").
- **Financial Settings:** Set custom financial year dates for accurate tax reporting.
- **Automated Backups:** Set up automatic weekly or monthly backups of your agency's data to keep it safe.
- **Custom Emailing:** Connect your own email address to securely send invoices directly to clients from within the app.
- **Superadmin Portal:** A master dashboard for the platform owner to approve new agency demo requests and oversee all workspaces.

### 👥 Master Directories & Team
Keep permanent, organized lists of:
- **Customers & Suppliers:** Your clients and the B2B companies you buy from.
- **Airlines & Services:** The specific flights and services (Visas, Hotels, Tour Packages) you sell.
- **Bank Accounts:** Track physical cash registers, digital wallets (like UPI), and corporate bank accounts.
- **Staff Members:** Add your sales agents and assign them to specific invoices to track their individual sales performance.

### 💰 Selling to Customers
- **Invoices:** Create detailed bills with multiple items (e.g., flight + hotel). The system secretly tracks your buying cost versus the selling price to automatically calculate your profit.
- **Receipts & Digital Wallets:** Log payments received from customers. If a customer overpays or gives a large advance, the extra money is kept in a "digital wallet" (Unallocated Advance) to be easily used for their next trip.
- **Customer Reconciliation:** A smart tool that automatically takes a customer's large advance payment and applies it to their oldest unpaid bills to clear their debts instantly.

### 💸 Buying from Suppliers
- **Supplier Ledger:** Track exactly how much money you owe to airlines and aggregators.
- **Payments:** Log payments sent to suppliers to automatically reduce your outstanding debts.
- **Supplier Reconciliation:** Easily map advance payments you made to a supplier against future bills they send you.

### 🔄 Cancellations, Refunds & Bonuses
- **Refund Vouchers:** When a trip is cancelled, safely manage the refund coming back from the airline and the smaller refund going back to the customer. The system automatically secures the difference as your cancellation fee profit.
- **Incentives:** Track extra backend bonuses and commissions you receive directly from airlines or DMCs for hitting sales targets.

### 📊 Expenses & Bank Transfers
- **Expenses:** Log daily running costs (Office Rent, Salaries) by category to automatically deduct them from your net profit.
- **Contra (Transfers):** Move money between accounts (e.g., depositing physical cash into the HDFC bank account) without messing up your income or expense sheets.

### 📈 Live Dashboard & Reports
- **Real-Time Dashboard:** See how much money you made today, your live bank balances, and a bar chart comparing your sales versus your expenses.
- **Comprehensive Reports Suite:** Instantly view your overall Profit & Loss Statement, Balance Sheet, and Ageing Reports (who owes you the most money and who you owe money to).
- **Ledgers & Statements:** Generate detailed, printable statements that show every transaction with a specific customer or supplier.
- **PDF Invoices:** Instantly download customized, branded invoices as PDF files to print or share.
